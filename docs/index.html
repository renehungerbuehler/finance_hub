Features

Everything you need to track and grow wealth

Built around real Swiss financial workflows — with AI that reads your documents so you don't have to type everything in manually.

📊

Live Portfolio P&L

Real-time prices via Yahoo Finance for all ETF, stock, and crypto positions. Cost basis, current value, and P&L per position and account.

🎯

Budget Scenarios

Model any number of income configurations. Link expenses, toggle insurance and subscriptions. Cashflow split donut updates live. PDF export per scenario.

🤖

AI Finance Advisor

Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini — or a fully local model via Ollama. Your full financial context is auto-injected. Ask anything, or tap a ready-made quick-action. Pin any answer directly to your Dashboard as a permanent note.

📅

Wealth Tracker

Month-by-month portfolio grid. Forecast per account, sync actual balances, and visualise forecast vs real growth across the year.

📋

Swiss Tax History

Multi-year records with all 4 Swiss tax types (Staats-/Gemeinde + Bundessteuer × Provisorisch/Schlussrechnung) with paid dates and bar chart.

📤

Tax Report Export

One-click export of a structured tax report with all account balances, investment positions, income, insurance premiums, and deductions — ready to hand to your accountant or paste into your tax return.

💡

FIRE Calculator

Financial freedom target using the 4% rule adjusted for Swiss wealth tax. Shows both the capital needed (money system) and the monthly revenue needed (business system).

📂

AI-Powered File Import

Drop a PDF, photo, or spreadsheet onto any section — accounts, subscriptions, recurring costs, insurance, or taxes — and the AI reads it and fills in the data automatically. Customise the extraction prompt per category for best results.

🔒

100% Private — incl. Local AI

Runs entirely on your own hardware via Docker. No cloud sync, no third-party storage. Use Ollama to run a local LLM (e.g. Llama 3.3, Mistral) — not a single byte of your financial data leaves your machine. Full JSON backup and restore from the sidebar.

Pages

Seven pages, one complete picture

Every page feeds into the others — and every section supports AI file import, so you can drop a document anywhere to update your data.

🏠 Dashboard overview

Net worth with liquid/locked split, survival runway, cashflow chart, portfolio donut, and key stats. AI quick-action buttons launch instant analysis. Pin any AI answer as a permanent card right here.

🏦 Accounts data entry

All accounts with balances, types (Checking, Investment, Crypto, Pension 2A/3A), and institutions. Inline editable and sortable. Upload a bank statement or screenshot and let AI fill in the balances.

📈 Portfolio live prices

Expandable per-account positions with real-time Yahoo Finance prices, shares, avg buy price, current value, and P&L. Allocation breakdown chart. AI-powered quick analysis with pinnable results.

🎯 Scenarios budgeting

Editable budget models with incomes, payroll deductions, linked expenses, savings targets, and investment allocations. Cashflow donut updates live. PDF export per scenario.

📊 Tracker monthly

Year grid with per-account forecast rows and actual balance cells. Sync from Accounts. Forecast vs result chart. Compound interest calculator.

💳 Expenses costs

Subscriptions, recurring costs, insurance policies, and multi-year tax records — all with AI import. Drop a bill, invoice, or payslip and the AI extracts the numbers. All amounts normalised to monthly and yearly CHF.

🧭 Strategy planning

Swiss 3+2 pillar framework, FIRE number (money system + business system), net hourly rate, and the indentured time calculator — see exactly what any purchase costs in working days.

Built for Switzerland

Swiss financial law, built in

Not a generic budgeting app adapted for Switzerland — the Swiss financial structure is the foundation.

🏛️

3-Pillar Pension System

AHV (Pillar 1), BVG / Pensionskasse (Pillar 2), Pillar 3a — tracked in accounts, scenarios, and strategy. Voluntary buy-in (Einkauf) logic in AI context.

📋

4 Swiss Tax Line Types

Staats-/Gemeinde + Bundessteuer × Provisorisch/Schlussrechnung per year, with paid dates and a multi-year bar chart overview.

💰

Tax-Free Capital Gains

Swiss private investors pay zero capital gains tax (Art. 16 Abs. 3 DBG). Reflected in FIRE calculations and fully explained to the AI advisor.

🩺

Krankenkasse Franchise

AI knows all franchise options (CHF 300–2,500), co-pay rules, break-even logic, and the October 31 switch deadline. Costs tracked in insurance.

🎯

FIRE Adjusted for Wealth Tax

Freedom target uses the 4% rule minus ~0.25% Vermögenssteuer (Kanton Zürich) = 3.75% net yield — built into every capital calculation.

🏢

Business & Freelance Target

Calculates the gross monthly revenue needed for financial freedom as a self-employed person — accounting for Swiss AHV contributions (~10%) and income tax (~25%).

🕐

Swiss Working Hours Standard

Net hourly rate calculated as net salary ÷ (40h × 45 working weeks), referencing the Swiss OR Art. 321c standard. Converts any purchase into real work time.

🇨🇭

CHF-Native Formatting

All amounts use de-CH locale with apostrophe separators: CHF 1'234'567. Currency-native throughout every page and chart.

Quick Start

Up and running in minutes

One script installs everything — Homebrew, Docker, all dependencies — and loads sample data automatically.

1

Clone the repo

Download Finance Hub from GitHub to your Mac.

2

Add an API key

Copy .env.example to .env and add your Anthropic, OpenAI, or Gemini key.

3

Run setup

One command installs all prerequisites, builds the app, and loads sample data.

4

Open the app

Visit http://localhost:3000 — done.

# Clone
git clone https://github.com/renehungerbuehler/finance_hub.git
cd finance_hub

# Configure your AI provider
cp .env.example .env
# Edit .env → add ANTHROPIC_API_KEY, OPENAI_API_KEY, or GEMINI_API_KEY

# One-command setup (installs Homebrew, Docker, make, jq — builds + loads sample data)
bash setup.sh

# Open http://localhost:3000
